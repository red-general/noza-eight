noza-eight

This is an openmovie cartoon project. The genre of this film is a sci-fi.

The story is about the war on the planet Noza-8 beetwen two cities "Ruby Town" and "Sapphirine City". Governors of the cities are Scarlet General and Cobalt General. They are twins. In the scenario you could get the answer to the question - "Why did they began the war?"
